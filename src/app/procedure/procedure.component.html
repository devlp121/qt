Publishing Procedures

rate_review

Review

We receive your manuscript and send it for peer review. The comments from peer review (as tracked changes or suggestions) are sent to the author through mail.
edit

Editing

Author makes necessary changes and sends the amended document back to the publisher who edits the manuscript if need be in accordance with the publisher’s editorial policy.
The document is then sent to the publisher’s proofreading department.
build

Design

The document is then sent for layout and cover design where produce a dummy book and send it to the author who may make final alterations if need be. The author then sends back the dummy and any alterations to the publisher who implements the changes as per the returned dummy book.
publish

Publishing

The publisher occasions the publication of the book/ journal article.
